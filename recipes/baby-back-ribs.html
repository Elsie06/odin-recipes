Baby Back Ribs

Description

These slow cooker ribs are the best - and turn out perfect every time!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds baby back ribs, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
    • <1i>clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Gathe all ingredients
  2. Season ribs with salt and pepper
  3. Pour 1/2 cup of water into the slow cooker, then add ribs. Scatter onion and garlic over top. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours
  4. When the ribs are almost finished, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  5. Transfer ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet; discard onion and garlic. Coat with barbeque sauce
  6. Bake ribs in the preheated oven until sauce caramelizes and sticks to meat, 10 to 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
